Description

An architect is a trained and licensed professional who specializes in the design and planning of buildings and structures. Their primary role is to create functional, aesthetically pleasing, and safe spaces that meet the needs and requirements of their clients. Here's a more detailed description of what architects do Design: Architects work closely with clients to understand their vision, needs, and objectives for a project. They develop design concepts and plans that incorporate both the client's preferences and local building codes and regulations.Drawings and Blueprints: Architects create detailed drawings and blueprints that outline the specifications, dimensions, and materials required for construction. These documents serve as a guide for contractors and builders.
